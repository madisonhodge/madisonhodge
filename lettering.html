lettering


The following illustrations were created using Adobe Illustrator. There are many quotes and song lyrics that inspire me in my everyday life, so I wanted to choose a few and make a collection to convey my thoughts. I chose a neutral color scheme that makes me think of calm and peaceful sunsets. I'm a huge fan of pastels, and love to use them in my work whenever I can.


Each piece envokes a different feeling in my mind, and reminds me of different lessons I need to remember. Being "made of magic" or even "stardust" is something I try to do continuously, because in my mind this means I am always creating and never giving up. I also chose lyrics from "Golden" by Harry Styles and "Golden Hour" by Kacey Musgraves, because the color palette makes me feel golden. Golden is something everyone should feel.
