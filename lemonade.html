branding + website mockups

I have not had many experiences in creating mockups and website designs on my own, so I decided to create one for my dream business: a lemonade cafe.

As a lover of all things lemonade, I thought about how I would want to create a website for a dream client. I chose bright yellows that contrast with blues and yellows for the business' color scheme. I also created a logo, and an alternate logo in Adobe Illustrator.
