digital collages

Collages are one of my favorite things to make. The entire process is fun and creative, from finding images, to thinking of ideas, to actually laying out the images and finalizing the design. I have been using Adobe Photoshop for a total of 4 years, and collages have always been my favorite creations for personal art and blogs.


Each of the collages below was created using stock photos off of websites such as Pexels or Unsplash. A few also include images that I took at concerts or the beach. The collages are meant to convey the beauty of nature and our imaginations. They also include a few of my favorite things and songs in life.
