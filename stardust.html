stardust design

Stardust Design is my current project that I am passionate about. I started this project on Instagram, where I post digital portraits and illustrations and connect with other creative artists. After posting on Instagram for a while and building a small following, I created an Etsy shop, where I offer custom digital illustrations, fun stickers, and hopefully more in the future!


I have loved being able to put all of myself into my small "side hustle" and make it what I've always dreamed of. Running a small art business has always been in the back of my mind, and although it is small right now and I'm still in the first steps, it's very exciting. Take a look around at the content I have created through Stardust, and also check out my Instagram and Etsy!
