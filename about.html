about me: madison hodge

I am a lover of pastels, web design, mood boards, butterflies, fonts, the ocean, mango dragonfruit lemonade, and so much more.


I am currently a student at Santa Fe College in Gainesville, FL pursuing an AS degree in Programming and Analysis. I plan to transfer in the future and achieve a Bachelor's in Advertising. In my future, I hope to gain an immense amount of knowledge about web design, how to skillfully market a business online, the key to creating logos, and more!


Stardust Illustrations is my current project, where I create and share digital portraits and illustrations. I have also opened an Etsy shop, where I sell custom digital illustrations, stickers, and hopefully more in the future. When I'm not working on projects or coursework, you can find me watching Food Network, at the drive-thru of Starbucks, pinning ideas on Pinterest, or just chilling with my family + boyfriend.
