digital portraits + illustrations

This year with my new found free time and urge to do something rather than nothing, I picked up a new hobby that I had been craving to learn: digital portraits. I've been familiar with basic digital art for as long as I can remember, but had never pursued getting better at it. So, I downloaded Adobe Fresco and began to practice.


I enjoy using reference photos from my everyday life, my favorite social media influencers/youtubers, and my favorite pins from Pinterest. I love to use pastel, but fun colors for backgrounds. I also love to focus on detailed shading when creating close-up portraits, but prefer to keep shading to a minimum when drawing a full body photo. I began an Instagram account to share my art, and I love to create, post, and celebrate other creators' artwork.
